About

Kate / Sassafras

Hi, I'm Kate, also known as Sassafras.

In 2018, I hiked the Appalachian Trail, solo. I developed serious nerve problems in my feet but still completed my hike.

In March of 2020, I contracted COVID. I soon developed what's called longhaul Covid or just Long Covid (I may abbreviate this as LC, too).

This site is my attempt to make sense of all those experiences and draw parallels between them, to share talks or other presentations I've given, and just generally to offer support to others facing challenging endurance efforts and chronic illnesses.

I believe we can all live our lives with a little more sass.

See Presentations with sass for recordings of talks I've given.

And if you're interested in having me give a talk in-person or virtually at your event or organization, please reach out!
