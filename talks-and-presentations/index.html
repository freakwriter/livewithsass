Talks & Presentations

See some of my talks and presentations, or request that I come give one for you!

I give talks on a range of topics, including:

  • End-of-Life Doulas: What do EOL Doulas/Death Doulas do, why would you want to use one, etc.
  • Long Covid: My experiences as a LC patient
  • Thru-hiking: My experiences thru-hiking the Appalachian Trail solo in 2018. I've most often given talks to high school students. :)
  • Thru-hiking or chronic illness metaphors in the workplace: endurance efforts and chronic illness can offer useful coping strategies and metaphors for workplace problems.

So far, not a lot of my talks have been recorded for resharing, but you can check out https://www.livewithsass.com/presentationswithsass for more information.
